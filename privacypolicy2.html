Privacy Policy SharpHomes

Welcome to SharpHomes, the mobile application developed by AI Enriches ("we", "us", "our"). We are dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users ("user", "you", "your"). This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of your information through our iOS and Android application.

Information Collection

SharpHomes values your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Users are not required to register or provide any personal data to use the app. All interactions are one-off and occur solely on your device.

Use of Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use or process any user data. The app functions by processing the address information you input to generate property evaluations, but this data is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Sharing of Information

As we do not collect or store any personal information, we do not share any user data with third parties.

Data Security

Your privacy is paramount. All data processing occurs locally on your device. We do not transmit any personal or address information over the internet, ensuring that your data remains secure and private.

Children's Privacy

SharpHomes is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no information for you to access, modify, or delete. Your use of the app is entirely anonymous.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
